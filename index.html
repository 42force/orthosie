Welcome to Orthosie.

Completely free Django-based point of sale system.

About

Orthosie was started as a project for the Bread and Roses Food Cooperative grocery store. It is intended to be a fully featured system for use by any small or medium sized retail business.

What it does

Right now, not that much. An inventory can be built up of items that are sold by UPC. It is possible to ring these items, accept payment for them, and print a reciept.

What it will do

  • Ring up items by either a UPC or PLU code.
  • Ring up bulk items.
  • Allow for many different users.
  • Allow for customers to be grouped and receive discounted prices based on the group or groups that they are in.
